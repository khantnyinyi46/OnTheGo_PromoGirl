OnTheGo_PromoGirl is an interactive mobile application that brings gamification into real-world promotions. Users spin a digital wheel containing real-life items, and when the wheel stops, they win the product in reality.

Core Features
🎡 Spinning Wheel Gameplay

A fun, engaging wheel with real products displayed.

Users spin the wheel directly in the app.

🎁 Real-Life Rewards

When the spin stops, the selected product is awarded to the user in real life.

Seamless connection between digital play and tangible rewards.

📲 User-Friendly Experience

Simple, intuitive interface for spinning and claiming rewards.

Encourages customer engagement and participation in events or promotions.

Benefits
Boosts customer engagement through gamification.

Connects digital interaction with real-world product distribution.

Provides businesses with a creative way to promote items and events.

Enhances user excitement and loyalty with instant, tangible rewards.
